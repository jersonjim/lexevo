# Why Lexevo?

## The Name

**Lexevo** = **Lex** (from *lexicon*, the body of vocabulary) + **evo** (from *evolve*)

## How We Got Here

We evaluated several names and checked availability on the App Store, Google Play, and trademark databases.

**Names ruled out:**

| Name | Reason |
|------|--------|
| Voca | 5+ apps on the App Store with that name, including direct competitors |
| Lexora | 3+ apps on Google Play and App Store with that exact name |
| VocabApp | Generic, no personality |

**Names that were available:** Lexevo, Wordevo, Lexbit, Hablex

We chose **Lexevo** because:

1. **Available** — no existing app, brand, or registered trademark found
2. **Meaningful** — communicates what the app does: build vocabulary (*lex*) and evolve (*evo*)
3. **Connects to the mascot** — the mascot that grows with your streak is the visual representation of "evo". The name and the core mechanic reinforce each other
4. **Pronounceable in all 3 supported languages** — Spanish, English, and Portuguese speakers can all say it naturally
5. **Short and memorable** — 6 letters, 3 syllables, no ambiguous spelling

## Where the Name Appears

- `app.json` — app name and slug (`lexevo`)
- `package.json` — package name
- `screens/ProfileScreen.tsx` — About modal (app name + copyright)
- `locales/en.json`, `es.json`, `pt.json` — About description text
