Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Lexevo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a vocabulary learning application developed by Lexevo Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Password — hashed and stored securely by Supabase. We never have access to your plain-text password.

Profile information (optional)

  • Nickname
  • Profile avatar (photo you choose from your device)
  • Chosen study plan (Bronze, Silver, or Golden)
  • Mascot theme preference

Study data

  • Vocabulary words and meanings you add
  • Study history — which box each word is in and when it was reviewed
  • Streak and statistics derived from your study activity

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment information (the app is free with no in-app purchases)
  • Any data from other apps on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the app's features:

  • Authenticate you and sync your data across devices
  • Save your vocabulary, study history, and preferences
  • Calculate your study streak and statistics

We do not use your data for advertising, do not sell it to third parties, and do not use it for any purpose beyond operating the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Lexevo uses Supabase (supabase.com) as its backend infrastructure. Supabase handles database storage, authentication, and file storage. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. You can review Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or social media platforms.

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at jersonjim@gmail.com and we will process the deletion within 30 days.

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Row-level security policies in our database ensure that each user can only access their own data.

7. Children's Privacy

Lexevo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jersonjim@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
